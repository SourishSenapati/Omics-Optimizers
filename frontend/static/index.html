The Science of Mechanism

Traditional predictive models often prioritize pattern recognition over biological reality. Our Physics-Informed Neural Network (PINN) bridge this gap by embedding the rigorous laws of compartment dynamics directly into the deep learning architecture.

By enforcing SIR Differential Equations during the learning phase, the engine identifies the hidden kinetic parameters—transmission velocity and recovery rate—ensuring forecasts are scientifically grounded and physically consistent.

Genomic Alignment

Establishing high-fidelity links between population-level transmission spikes and strain-specific molecular targets using the PubChem PUG-REST protocol.

Policy Simulation

An interactive, multi-variate sandbox for simulating non-pharmaceutical interventions (NPIs) to evaluate "flatten the curve" strategies in real-time.

ICD-11 Taxonomy

Standardized medical ontology integration ensuring 100% interoperability with WHO international disease classification registries.

Forensic Intelligence

Real-time NLP extraction of clinical alerts from ProMED-mail feeds for automated threat level assessment and location detection.
